Year 2 Block C

🔬 Research Methodology & Statistics

Developing rigorous research skills—learning to design experiments, analyze data statistically, and communicate findings with scientific precision and academic integrity.

📊 Statistics 🔍 Research Methods 📈 Probability 📝 Academic Writing

Project Overview

This block focused on the scientific foundations of data science research. Moving beyond coding and models, I learned how to formulate research questions, design valid experiments, and draw statistically sound conclusions.

These skills are essential for conducting meaningful data science work that can withstand scrutiny and contribute genuine insights.

Key Components

  • Research Methods: Understanding different research paradigms, formulating hypotheses, and designing studies that answer specific questions.
  • Quantitative Research: Statistical analysis, hypothesis testing, and interpreting numerical data with appropriate methods.
  • Qualitative Research: Interview techniques, thematic analysis, and understanding when qualitative approaches are appropriate.
  • Probability Theory: Deep dive into probability distributions, Bayesian thinking, and uncertainty quantification.
  • Academic Skills: Scientific writing, literature review, citation practices, and presenting research findings.

Statistical Methods Covered

The project covered a comprehensive range of statistical techniques:

  • Descriptive statistics and data exploration
  • Hypothesis testing (t-tests, ANOVA, chi-square)
  • Regression analysis and correlation
  • Non-parametric methods
  • Confidence intervals and effect sizes
  • Multiple comparison corrections
  • Power analysis and sample size determination

Research Project

The block culminated in conducting an independent research project:

  • Formulated a research question relevant to data science
  • Conducted literature review of existing work
  • Designed and executed data collection methodology
  • Applied appropriate statistical analyses
  • Wrote a formal research report with proper academic conventions
  • Presented findings to peers and faculty

Learning Outcomes

  • Design rigorous research studies with appropriate methodologies
  • Apply statistical methods to analyze quantitative data
  • Conduct and analyze qualitative research when appropriate
  • Communicate research findings in academic writing
  • Critically evaluate research quality and validity
